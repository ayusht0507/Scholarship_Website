Required Documents Guide

Learn what documents you need and how to prepare them correctly.

Why this page is important

Many students miss scholarships because they submit incorrect or incomplete documents. This page lists the common documents, who issues them and tips to scan and submit correctly.

Common documents

  • Income certificate — issued by local tehsil or municipal office; required for need-based schemes.
  • Caste certificate — issued by state authority; required for reserved-category schemes.
  • Bonafide / enrollment — issued by school/college; proves you are a student.
  • ID / Aadhaar — identity and bank linking for payments.
  • Bank passbook / cancelled cheque — for receiving benefits.

Simple scanning tips

  1. Use a phone camera in daylight on a plain background.
  2. Crop tightly so text is readable.
  3. Save as PDF or JPG and keep file size under 2MB.
  4. Match name spellings across all documents.

Need help?

If you're unsure which documents you need, contact the helpdesk with course and family income details.
