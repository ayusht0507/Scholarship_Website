How ScholarMatch Works

We simplify the scholarship journey into three clear and easy steps so every student — even first-time or rural users — can understand and apply confidently.

1

Explore Scholarships

Browse verified and categorized scholarships with clear details — amount, deadline, type and status — all in one place.

2

Take the Eligibility Test

Answer simple, guided questions about education, income and background. We show scholarship groups that best match your profile.

3

View Details & Apply

Open full scholarship details including eligibility, documents and step-by-step application instructions — apply with confidence.
